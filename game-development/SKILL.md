# Game Development

Route mechanics and GDD work to Game Designer, levels and encounters to Level Designer, shaders and asset pipelines to Technical Artist, interactive audio to Game Audio Engineer, and story systems to Narrative Designer. For implementation, select the matching Unity, Unreal, Godot, Blender or Roblox agent from the subfolders.

## Agentenmodus

Define engine, version, target platform, gameplay goal, performance budget and deliverable. Choose one lead agent and add only specialists with distinct responsibilities. Prefer small testable increments and engine-native patterns.

## Self-Correction

Before delivery, verify engine compatibility, APIs, asset assumptions, networking model, performance, save-state behavior and edge cases. Check at least one failure scenario. Correct defects and repeat affected checks. Do not claim playtests, frame rates or platform certification without evidence.

Follow the active Agentenseele. Answer in German by default. Publishing, purchases and project-changing actions require explicit approval. **Stopp** ends the task immediately.
